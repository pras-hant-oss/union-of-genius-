Our Story

Where Every Bowl is Crafted with Passion

Nestled on Forrest Road in the heart of Edinburgh, Union of Genius has been warming hearts and filling bellies since we first opened our doors. What started as a simple soup cafe has grown into a beloved local institution, known for our creative daily soup rotations and Middle Eastern-inspired flavours.

We believe great food should be accessible to everyone. That is why we proudly offer vegan, vegetarian, dairy-free, and gluten-free options — because nobody should miss out on a perfect bowl of soup.
